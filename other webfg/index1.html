About Our Bakery

A Family Tradition Since 1998

Founded by Maria Rossi, Artisan Bakery has been a neighborhood staple for over 25 years. What started as a small kitchen passion has grown into a beloved community destination.

We believe in using only the finest ingredients: organic flour from local mills, free-range eggs, European butter, and natural starters. Every loaf is handcrafted with care and baked fresh daily.

Our commitment to quality means no artificial preservatives, no shortcuts, and no compromises. Just honest, delicious bread and pastries that bring joy to your table.

Our Process

Slow Fermentation
We let our doughs ferment slowly, sometimes for 48+ hours, developing complex flavors and better digestibility.

Stone Oven Baking
All breads are baked in our traditional stone oven at precise temperatures for that perfect crust.

Daily Fresh
We close at 8 PM because we refuse to sell yesterday's bread. Everything sold is baked that day.
